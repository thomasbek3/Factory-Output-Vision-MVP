# PRICING — Factory Vision Output Counter

---

## 1) Pricing Philosophy

Sell hardware at slim margin to get the box in the door.
Charge monthly SaaS for the software intelligence.
Upsell tiers as features ship.
Never let a factory's counting system stop working because of a billing issue.

## 8) 10x Value Rule Check

The SaaS 10x rule: value delivered should be ≥10x the price charged.

At $49/mo ($588/yr):
- One undetected 30-minute line stop costs $500–$5,000 in lost production
- Catching ONE stop per month = 10-100x ROI
- Catching a drop pattern early saves hours of lost output per week
- Operator absence accountability alone justifies the cost for many factories

At $99/mo ($1,188/yr):
- OEE visibility typically drives 5-15% productivity improvement
- On a line producing $50K/month in output, 5% improvement = $2,500/month
- That's 25x ROI on a $99 investment

The pricing is defensible at every tier.

## 9) Free Trial Strategy

- 30 days free with hardware purchase
- Full-feature access during trial (highest tier)
- At end of trial: auto-downgrades to Counter tier unless they choose to upgrade
- No credit card required for trial (invoice after 30 days)
- Hardware is non-refundable after 30 days (they own the box regardless)

Target: Vorne converts 92.8% of trials. We should aim for 80%+ by making the first 30 days prove undeniable value.

## 10) What We Don't Charge For

- Software updates (included in all tiers)
- Basic email support (included in all tiers)
- Multiple users accessing the web UI (unlimited, no per-seat pricing)
- Data storage on the local device (included)
- Data export (CSV, API where available by tier)

Factories hate per-user pricing and hidden fees. Keep it dead simple: one price per line, everything included for that tier.

---

## 11) Pricing Principles

1. **Per-line pricing scales naturally.** Small factory = small bill. Big factory = big bill. No negotiation needed.
2. **Hardware gets the box on the floor.** Slim margin is fine. Recurring revenue is the business.
3. **Annual discount locks in commitment.** Pay 10 months, get 12 — standard SaaS, factory-friendly.
4. **Volume discount rewards expansion.** Every line they add makes the per-line cost drop, encouraging full-factory adoption.
5. **Never kill the count.** If a factory falls behind on payment, degrade gracefully (disable reports/alerts) but never stop the count. A factory that loses trust in the count will never come back.
6. **Price to the clipboard, not to Vorne.** Our competitor is "nothing," not Vorne. $49/mo needs to feel like an obvious yes compared to flying blind.
